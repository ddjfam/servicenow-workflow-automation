In ServiceNow environments, strong product ownership requires clarity around intake, prioritization, out-of-box capabilities, stakeholder needs, and release planning.

## Product Ownership Approach

My approach to workflow automation product ownership includes:

1. Understanding the business problem before designing the solution
2. Defining workflows that are clear, usable, and scalable
3. Prioritizing enhancements based on impact, effort, and urgency
4. Using platform capabilities where possible before pursuing customization
5. Writing clear requirements that reduce ambiguity for delivery teams
6. Measuring success through adoption, efficiency, and workflow outcomes
